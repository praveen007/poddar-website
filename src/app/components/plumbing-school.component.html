Ashirvad Plumbing School

Enriching local communities through holistic knowledge transfers

At Ashirvad, we believe that real success lies in creating real impact towards the betterment of society. With this noble goal in mind, we have undertaken an initiative to provide hands-on training to amateurs and young professionals in plumbing technology - industry best practices, occupational health and safety, and water and energy conservation. This drive is a joint enterprise in association with the Indian Plumbing Skill Sector Council (IPSC) and the Skill India initiative by the Central Government.
