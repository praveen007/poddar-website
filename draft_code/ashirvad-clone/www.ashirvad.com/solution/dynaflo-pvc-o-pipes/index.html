Design and Specifications/ USP

Exceptional Durability and Long-Lasting Performance

Dynaflo PVC-O pipes are engineered to last for more than 100 years. This durability is achieved through multiple factors, including the inherent strength of PVC-O material and a meticulous manufacturing process. Additionally, it’s smooth interior wall minimizes pressure loss within system, thereby reducing the energy required for water transportation.

Advanced Molecular Orientation Technology

Ashirvad Dynaflo pipes leverage a cutting-edge process known as Molecular Orientation which realigns the PVC molecule structure, creating a highly organized and layered pipe wall. This enhances the pipe's mechanical properties, resulting in superior resistance to impact and stress during both installation and operation.

Commitment to Water Quality and Long-Term Integrity

Ashirvad Dynaflo pipes are specifically designed to safeguard the quality of the water they transport. PVC-O material effectively resist corrosion, eliminating the need for additional coatings or protective measures that could potentially contaminate the water. This ensures that the water delivered through Dynaflo pipes consistently meets the highest standards.

Why Ashirvad Dynaflo?

Unmatched Strength and Resource Efficiency

Ashirvad Dynaflo pipes boast exceptional impact resistance, minimizing the risk of breakage during installation and ensuring a longer lifespan. Additionally, the optimized structure of these pipes, achieved through the Molecular Orientation process, allows for the use of less material compared to conventional options. This translates to a more resource-efficient solution that minimizes environmental impact.

Superior Flow Rates for Enhanced Efficiency

The meticulously designed smooth interior surface of Ashirvad Dynaflo pipes significantly minimizes friction within the system. This reduction in friction translates to a remarkable increase in flow capacity, ranging from 15% to 40% more flow compared to pipes constructed from other materials. This translates to a more efficient water transmission system, allowing you to deliver the required water volume with a smaller pipe diameter or achieve a higher flow rate using the same pipe size.

Sustainable Choice for a Greener Future

Ashirvad Dynaflo pipes are champions of environmental responsibility. The smooth interior design minimizes energy consumption throughout the pipe's operational life by reducing friction and pressure loss. Furthermore, the inherent durability of these pipes translates to a longer lifespan, reducing the need for frequent replacements and associated environmental burdens. Additionally, the manufacturing process of Ashirvad Dynaflo pipes is optimized to minimize CO2 emissions, contributing to a greener future.

FAQ

Ashirvad Dynaflo pipes has an exceptional lifespan exceeding 100 years, provided they are installed and operated correctly.

The innovative push-fit joint system utilizes a watertight seal designed to prevent leaks. This seal incorporates a polypropylene ring and a synthetic rubber lip, ensuring exceptional joint stability and peace of mind for your water transmission system.

The spigot mark plays a vital role during installation. It indicates the precise depth to which the male end of the pipe needs to be inserted into the socket of another pipe to create a secure and watertight connection. Following this designated insertion depth ensures optimal performance and longevity of your water transmission system.

For proper storage and to prevent damage, Ashirvad Dynaflo pipes should be laid horizontally on a flat surface with sufficient spacing between each pipe. Additionally, provide support structures at regular intervals, typically every 1.5 meters, to prevent the pipes from bowing. When stacking pipes in layers, alternate the socket and free ends to avoid placing any unnecessary stress on the socket connections.
